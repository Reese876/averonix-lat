How It Works

Getting started with Averonix is incredibly simple:

  1. Install the Extension: Add Averonix to your Chrome browser from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Navigate to YouTube: Go to any YouTube video you want to process
  3. Extract Content: Click the Averonix icon and select "Get Transcript" or "Get Summary"
  4. Export & Use: Choose your preferred format and download your content

Advanced Features

Our auto-seek feature automatically highlights the current section of the transcript as the video plays, making it easy to follow along and take notes in real-time.

For long videos, Averonix automatically breaks content into manageable sections, making it easier to navigate and reference specific information.
